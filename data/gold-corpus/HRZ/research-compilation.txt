## Company Identity
### overview
Horizon Minerals Limited (ASX: HRZ) is a Western Australian gold company building a multi-asset production platform in the Eastern Goldfields. The hub-and-spoke strategy aggregates multiple deposits within trucking distance of existing processing infrastructure in the Kalgoorlie region. The flagship Boorara project is at advanced development stage with planned toll treatment production. The Nimbus silver-zinc project provides diversification beyond pure gold exposure.

## Narrative
### theNarrative
Horizon Minerals is a <strong>gold developer with genuine production optionality</strong> in one of the world's premier gold mining districts. The investment case rests on two pillars: <strong>gold price sensitivity</strong> (price-sensitive play on AUD gold above A$4,000/oz) and <strong>production transition</strong> (first gold at Boorara re-rates from developer to producer multiples). The hub-and-spoke model: aggregating multiple deposits within trucking distance of Kalgoorlie's processing plants: reduces capital requirements by <span class="key-stat">A$50-100M</span> versus a standalone plant. The stock has re-rated <span class="key-stat">+94%</span> from its 52-week low, suggesting the market is beginning to price in the production thesis.

### evidenceCheck
Gold price macro thesis (N1) well-supported by central bank buying and AUD weakness. Production transition (N2) relies on management execution and toll treatment economics not yet confirmed. Funding risk (N3) is the key bear case: pre-revenue companies invariably need capital.

### narrativeStability
MODERATE: Primarily driven by gold price and project milestones. A sustained gold correction or failed capital raise would fundamentally shift the thesis.
